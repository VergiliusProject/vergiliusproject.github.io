_LDR_DATA_TABLE_ENTRY

Windows 10 2009 20H2 (October 2020 Update) x86
Windows 10 2104 21H1 (May 2021 Update) x86
Windows 10 2110 21H2 (November 2021 Update) x86

        struct
        {
            ULONG PackagedBinary:1;                                         //0x34
